## Trial Period

### How the Trial Works

- New workspaces get a **14-day free trial**
- Full access to all features during trial
- No credit card required to start

### Trial Status

When you're in trial:
- "Trial ends in X days" message appears
- All features are available
- You'll be prompted to subscribe before expiration

### After Trial Ends

When the trial expires:
- Choose a subscription plan
- Add payment information
- Continue without interruption

## Managing Your Subscription

### Subscribing (After Trial)

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Review available plans
3. Click **"Subscribe"** on your chosen plan
4. Enter payment information
5. Confirm subscription

### Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Find the higher-tier plan
3. Click **"Upgrade"**
4. Confirm the change
5. Prorated billing applies

### Downgrading Your Plan

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Find the lower-tier plan
3. Click **"Downgrade"**
4. Verify you're within the new limits
5. Change takes effect at next billing cycle

> âš ï¸ **Note:** You cannot downgrade if you exceed the lower plan's limits. Reduce users or units first.

## Payment Management

### Updating Payment Method

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing**
2. Click **"Manage Billing"** or **"Update Payment"**
3. You'll be redirected to the Stripe customer portal
4. Update your card or payment method
5. Save changes

### Viewing Invoices

1. Access the Stripe customer portal via Billing page
2. View invoice history
3. Download invoices for records

### Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
1. Your status changes to "Past Due"
2. You receive email notification
3. Update your payment method promptly
4. Avoid service interruption

## Cancellation

### How to Cancel

## Billing FAQ

### When am I charged?

- Monthly on your subscription anniversary date
- Trial period is free
- First charge after trial ends

### Can I change plans anytime?

- Yes, upgrades take effect immediately
- Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle
- Prorated credits/charges apply

### What payment methods are accepted?

- Major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, Amex)
- Payment processing via Stripe

### Is my payment information secure?

- Payments processed by Stripe
- PCI-compliant
- Uplio doesn't store card numbers

### What if I need a custom plan?

Contact Uplio support for:
- Enterprise requirements
- Custom user/unit limits
- Volume discounts
- Special billing arrangements
