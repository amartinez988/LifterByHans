# Team Management

Team management lets you invite colleagues to your Uplio workspace, manage their access levels, and control permissions. This is how you bring your office staff, supervisors, and other team members into the system.

---

## Accessing Team Management

1. Click **"Team"** under the **People** section in the sidebar
2. You'll see current members and pending invites

## Inviting New Members

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1. On the Team page, find the **Invite new member** section
2. Enter the person's **email address**
3. Select a **role** (Admin or Member)
4. Click **"Create invite"**

> ğŸ“¸ *Screenshot: Invite form*

### What Happens Next

1. An invite is created with a unique link
2. The invite appears in **Pending Invites**
3. You can copy and share the link
4. The invitee clicks the link to join

### Invite Expiration

- Invites expire after a set period (typically 7 days)
- Expired invites can be revoked and recreated
- Invites show their expiration date

---

## Sharing Invite Links

### Copying the Link

1. Find the pending invite
2. Click the **Copy** button
3. Paste into email, message, or other communication

### What to Tell Invitees

Include in your message:
- The invite link
- Explanation of what Uplio is
- What they'll be able to do
- Any next steps after joining

Example message:
> "Hi! I've invited you to join our Uplio workspace. Click this link to create your account and access our elevator service management system: [link]"

## Revoking Invites

If you need to cancel an invite:

1. Find the invite in Pending Invites
2. Click **"Revoke"** button
3. Confirm the revocation

The invite link will no longer work.

### When to Revoke

- Invite sent to wrong email
- Person no longer needs access
- You want to change the role
- Security concern

---

## Managing Member Roles

### Changing a Role

1. Find the member in Current Members
2. Click **"Make admin"** or **"Make member"**
3. The role updates immediately

### Upgrading to Admin

Click **"Make admin"** to give someone:
- Full feature access
- Ability to create and edit records
- Team management access
- No billing access

### Downgrading to Member

Click **"Make member"** to restrict someone to:
- View-only access
- Cannot create or edit records
- Cannot manage team

---

## Removing Members

### How to Remove

1. Find the member in Current Members
2. Click **"Remove"** button
3. Confirm the removal

### What Happens

- They immediately lose access
- Their past activity is preserved
- They can be re-invited later

### Cannot Remove

- Yourself (you're logged in)
- The workspace owner

## Best Practices

### Role Assignment

- **Owner**: Only one, typically the business owner
- **Admin**: Office managers, dispatchers, supervisors
- **Member**: Mechanics who need read-only access

### Onboarding New Members

1. Create the invite with appropriate role
2. Copy and share the link directly
3. Explain what Uplio is and how you use it
4. Walk them through key features
5. Set up their notification preferences

### Security

- Revoke access promptly when someone leaves
- Use appropriate roles (don't make everyone Admin)
- Regularly review the member list
- Keep owner credentials secure

### Email Consistency

If someone is both a mechanic and a team member:
- Use the same email for both
- This enables personalized Technician View
- Keeps records connected

## Troubleshooting

### Invite Not Received

- Check spam/junk folders
- Verify the email address is correct
- Try revoking and re-creating
- Copy the link manually and send directly

### Can't Accept Invite

- Link may be expired
- Account may already exist with different email
- Try a different browser
- Contact the workspace admin

### Wrong Role Assigned

- Admin can change roles immediately
- No need to remove and re-invite
- Just click the appropriate role button
