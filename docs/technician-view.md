# Technician View

The Technician View is a mobile-optimized interface designed for field mechanics. It provides quick access to today's jobs, upcoming work, and emergency alerts in a streamlined, touch-friendly format.

2. Or navigate directly to `/technician`

## Today's Schedule

The main section shows jobs scheduled for today:

### Schedule Section

- **Section Header** ‚Äî "Today's Schedule" with link to full view
- **Job Cards** ‚Äî Individual jobs with details
- **Empty State** ‚Äî "All caught up!" message when no jobs remain

### Job Cards

Each job card displays:
- **Status Badge** ‚Äî Color-coded current status
- **Unit Identifier** ‚Äî Which unit the job is for
- **Building Name** ‚Äî Location
- **Scheduled Time** ‚Äî When the job should occur
- **Job Type** ‚Äî Maintenance, Inspection, etc.

### Status Colors

| Color | Status | Meaning |
|-------|--------|---------|
| üîµ Blue | SCHEDULED | Ready to start |
| üü° Yellow | EN_ROUTE | On the way |
| üü† Orange | ON_SITE | Currently working |

Completed and cancelled jobs don't show in this view.

### Tapping a Job

Tap any job card to:
1. View full job details
2. Update job status
3. Add notes
4. See complete information

## Mechanic Matching

The Technician View automatically shows relevant jobs:

1. **If you're a mechanic** ‚Äî Only your assigned jobs appear
2. **If you're not linked to a mechanic** ‚Äî All jobs may appear
3. **Matching is by email** ‚Äî Your user email matches the mechanic email

> üí° **Tip:** To see only your jobs, make sure your user email matches your mechanic profile email.

## Updating Job Status

### From Job Details

1. Tap a job card
2. You'll be taken to the job detail page
3. Update status using the form
4. Save changes

### Status Progression

Typical workflow:
```
SCHEDULED ‚Üí EN_ROUTE ‚Üí ON_SITE ‚Üí COMPLETED
```

Update your status as you:
1. Leave for the job site (EN_ROUTE)
2. Arrive at the location (ON_SITE)
3. Complete the work (COMPLETED)
