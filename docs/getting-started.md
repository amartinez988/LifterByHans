## Your First Steps

After onboarding, follow these steps to set up your workspace:

### Step 1: Add Your First Management Company

Management companies are the property managers you provide service for.

1. Click **"Companies"** in the sidebar (under Assets)
2. Click **"Add management company"**
3. Fill in the company details
4. Click **"Create company"**

> ðŸ“– See [Management Companies](./assets/companies.md) for detailed instructions.

### Step 2: Add Buildings

Buildings are the physical locations where your elevators are installed.

1. Open the management company you just created
2. Click **"Add building"**
3. Enter the building name and address
4. Click **"Create building"**

> ðŸ“– See [Buildings](./assets/buildings.md) for detailed instructions.

### Step 3: Add Units (Elevators/Escalators)

Units are the individual elevators or escalators you service.

1. Open the building you just created
2. Click **"Add unit"**
3. Fill in unit details (identifier, category, status, etc.)
4. Click **"Create unit"**

> ðŸ“– See [Units](./assets/units.md) for detailed instructions.

### Step 4: Add Your Mechanics

Mechanics are your field technicians who perform service work.

1. Click **"Mechanics"** in the sidebar (under People)
2. Click **"Add mechanic"**
3. Enter their name, contact info, and skill level
4. Click **"Create mechanic"**

> ðŸ“– See [Mechanics](./people/mechanics.md) for detailed instructions.

### Step 5: Invite Team Members

Bring your office staff into Uplio.

1. Click **"Team"** in the sidebar (under People)
2. Click **"Create invite"**
3. Enter the team member's email and role
4. Share the invite link with them

## Common Questions

### Can I change my company name later?

Yes, you can update your company information in Settings.

### What if I was invited to join an existing workspace?

If you received an invite link:
1. Click the invite link in your email
2. Create your account (or log in if you have one)
3. You'll automatically join the workspace with the assigned role

### Can I belong to multiple workspaces?

Currently, each user belongs to one workspace. Contact support if you need multi-workspace access.
