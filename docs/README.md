![Uplio Logo](../public/icon.svg)

---

## What is Uplio?

Uplio is a comprehensive elevator and escalator service management solution that helps elevator service companies:

- **Track Assets** — Manage buildings, units, and management companies in one place
- **Schedule & Dispatch** — Plan jobs, assign mechanics, and monitor progress in real-time
- **Stay Compliant** — Track inspections, certifications, and expiration dates
- **Respond to Emergencies** — Log and manage emergency calls efficiently
- **Engage Customers** — QR codes on units let building users report issues instantly
- **Collaborate** — Invite team members with role-based access control

## Quick Start Guide

1. **Sign Up** — Create your account at the Uplio login page
2. **Create Your Workspace** — Name your company during onboarding
3. **Add a Management Company** — Start by adding your first customer
4. **Add Buildings** — Create buildings under the management company
5. **Add Units** — Register elevators and escalators in each building
6. **Invite Your Team** — Bring in mechanics and office staff
7. **Start Scheduling** — Create jobs and dispatch technicians
